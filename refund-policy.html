Last updated: January 1, 2025

1. Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds are only considered under the following conditions:

  • If the service requested could not be processed or completed due to issues on our end.

  • If the service provided is significantly different from what was promised in terms of scope or delivery.

2. Non-Refundable Charges

The following charges are non-refundable:

  • Service execution fees for completed requests, including new connections, plan upgrades, cancellations, and bill payments.

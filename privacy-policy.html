We collect both personal and non-personal information to provide and improve our services.

a. Personal Information:

This is information that identifies you as an individual, including but not limited to:

  • Name and contact information
  • Email address
  • Phone number

b. Non-Personal Information:

This includes information that does not directly identify you, such as:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version

c. What We Do NOT Collect:

We never request or store:

  • Service provider account passwords
  • Social Security numbers or government ID numbers
  • Financial account credentials
  • Any sensitive information beyond what is necessary to facilitate your service requests

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve our guidance and assistance services.
  • To process your service requests and payments for our assistance fees.
  • To communicate with you regarding your account and service requests.
  • To send updates about our services (with your consent).
  • To analyze website usage to improve user experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information under the following circumstances:

a. With Third-Party Service Providers:

We may work with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website, processing payments, or fulfilling service requests. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for the purposes we authorize.

b. Legal Requirements:

We may disclose your information if required by law, in response to legal processes such as subpoenas or court orders, or to protect our rights and safety.

c. Business Transfers:

In the event of a merger, acquisition, bankruptcy, or sale of our business or assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will provide notice of such changes.

d. With Your Consent:

We may share your information with other parties when you explicitly request or authorize us to do so.

4. Your Privacy Rights & Choices

You have control over your personal information:

  • Access & Update: You can access and update your personal information by contacting us via phone or email.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of receiving promotional communications by following the unsubscribe link in our emails or contacting us directly.
  • Cookies: You can disable cookies in your browser settings; however, this may affect your website experience.
  • Data Deletion: You may request deletion of your personal information, subject to applicable legal requirements.

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encryption, firewalls, and secure payment processing. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

GetSetNet is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will promptly take steps to delete it.
